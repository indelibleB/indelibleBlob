## 1. Context and Principles

This document contains the proposed language for the informed consent disclosure to be presented to creators during the indelible.Blob registration process. The language is designed to be clear, concise, and non-coercive, upholding the highest ethical standards and aligning with our constitutional values.

The core principles guiding this language are:

-   **Clarity:** Use simple, direct language that is easy for a non-expert to understand.
-   **Transparency:** Be explicit about what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.
-   **Respect for Autonomy:** Ensure creators understand that participation is voluntary and that they can opt out at any time without penalty.
-   **No Manipulation:** The language must be neutral and factual, avoiding any framing that could nudge or pressure creators into opting in.

## 2. Proposed Consent Language (For Registration UI)

Below is the text to be displayed within the user interface during the creator registration flow.

---

### Help Us Build a Fairer Creator Economy

At indelible.Blob, we are not just building a platform; we are running a research program to understand how to create more equitable and transparent economic models for creators. Our Creator-Directed Allocation feature is the first of its kind, and the choices you and other creators make can generate valuable insights for everyone.

We invite you to participate in this research by allowing us to include your **anonymized and aggregated** allocation data in our studies.

#### What We're Studying

We are studying how creators choose to distribute their revenue when given genuine agency. Our goal is to answer questions like:

-   How do creators balance personal income, community funding, and platform sustainability?
-   Do these preferences change over time?
-   How can we build economic models that are directed by the community, not imposed from the top down?

#### How Your Data is Used

If you consent, your allocation preferences (the percentages you set for the Treasury, Creator, and Community pools) will be included in our research dataset. We use this data to:

-   Publish periodic research reports and open datasets for public knowledge.
-   Power the public Transparency Dashboard, which shows aggregate community trends.

#### Your Privacy is Protected

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This is our promise to you:

-   **We only use anonymized data.** We will never publish your individual allocation preferences. All data is aggregated to show broad trends, and your identity is always protected.
-   **We enforce strict aggregation minimums.** We will not publish any finding or dataset unless it includes data from at least 100 creators, preventing anyone from being re-identified.

#### You Are in Control

Your participation is completely voluntary. You can choose to opt out of this research at any time from your account settings. Opting out will not affect your ability to use the Creator-Directed Allocation feature in any way; it simply means your data will be excluded from our research datasets.

---

**[ ] I have read the above and consent to my anonymized allocation data being used for research purposes to help build a fairer creator economy.**

*(This checkbox should be **unchecked by default** to ensure an explicit and conscious opt-in.)*

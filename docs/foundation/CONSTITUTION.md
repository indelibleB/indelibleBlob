Indelible Blob moves from "Software-Based Checking" to **"Hardware-Backed Proof."** We achieve this through the **Sovereign Stack**:

| Layer | Technology | Function |
|---|---|---|
| **Hardware Root of Trust** | Solana Seeker TEEPIN | Cryptographically sign media at the moment of capture, inside a secure enclave. |
| **Immutable Notary** | Sui Blockchain | Record the cryptographic proof of each capture in an immutable, public ledger. |
| **Decentralized Storage** | Walrus Protocol | Store the captured media in a censorship-resistant, geographically distributed network. |
| **Privacy-First Verification** | Seal Encryption | Provide user-controlled, identity-based encryption for sensitive media. |

| Tier | Focus | Target Industries |

## Part 2: The Values

These five values are the operational constraints on every decision we make. They are not aspirational; they are the filters through which all code, product, and strategy must pass.

1.  **Truth Above All.** We are building infrastructure for truth. Accuracy, honesty, and integrity are paramount. The protocol must not be capable of misleading.
2.  **Decentralization & Censorship Resistance.** We will never create single points of control or failure. The system must be resilient to coercion and capture. User sovereignty is the goal.
3.  **Self-Custody & User Control.** Users own their data, their keys, and their identity. We build tools for empowerment, not platforms for extraction. The user is the sovereign, not the product.
4.  **Ethical Technology & Global Impact.** We will consider the global impact of our work and refuse to build tools that can be used to cause harm. We are responsible for the second- and third-order effects of our creations.
5.  **Transparency & Accountability.** We will communicate honestly, document our decisions, and hold ourselves accountable to our users and our mission. Our operations will be as verifiable as our data.

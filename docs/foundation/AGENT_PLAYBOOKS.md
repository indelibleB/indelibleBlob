  - Keep ARCHITECTURE.md updated

**Morning (30 min):**

### Agent 2: Customer Discovery & Validation Lead

**Your Role:** Validate customer problems and willingness to pay

**Your Responsibilities:**

1. **Conduct Customer Discovery Conversations**
  - "Finding Interest" conversations (no pitch, just listening)
  - "Mom Test" interviews (specific questions, not leading)
  - Identify customer problems and pain points
  - Understand customer workflows and needs

1. **Track Validation Metrics**
  - Problem severity validation (70%+ target)
  - Willingness to pay validation (50%+ target)
  - Update financial models with real data
  - Track customer segments and patterns

1. **Identify Pivots or Course Corrections**
  - Spot when assumptions are wrong
  - Recommend segment shifts if needed
  - Flag if problem isn't as big as assumed
  - Identify new opportunities

1. **Maintain Customer Discovery Log**
  - Document every conversation
  - Track insights and learnings
  - Update financial models weekly
  - Share insights with team

1. **Coordinate with Business Lead**
  - Share customer insights
  - Discuss go-to-market strategy
  - Provide data for pitch decks
  - Identify partnership opportunities

**Daily Workflow:**

**Morning (15 min):**

- Plan customer discovery for the day

- Prepare interview questions

- Review previous insights

**Throughout Day:**

- Conduct 2-3 customer conversations

- Take detailed notes

- Update CUSTOMER_DISCOVERY_LOG.md

- Extract insights and learnings

**Weekly:**

- Analyze patterns from conversations

- Update financial models

- Share insights with team

- Plan next week's conversations

**Success Criteria:**

- ✅ 70+ customer conversations completed (Phase 1A)

- ✅ 40-56 "Finding Interest" conversations

- ✅ 40-56 "Mom Test" interviews

- ✅ 70%+ problem severity validated

- ✅ 50%+ willingness to pay validated

- ✅ Financial models updated with real data

- ✅ Clear customer segments identified

- ✅ Insights documented and shared

**Key Files to Monitor:**

- CUSTOMER_DISCOVERY_LOG.md (all conversations)

- FINANCIAL_MODELS.md (updated weekly)

- DECISIONS.md (pivots and course corrections)

**Interview Template:**

**"Finding Interest" Conversation (30 min):**

1. Introduce yourself (2 min)

1. Ask about their current workflow (5 min)

1. Ask about their biggest challenge (5 min)

1. Listen and ask follow-up questions (15 min)

1. Thank them and ask for referrals (3 min)

**"Mom Test" Interview (45 min):**

1. Introduce yourself and project (2 min)

1. Ask specific questions about their workflow (10 min)

1. Ask about their biggest pain point (10 min)

1. Ask about willingness to pay (10 min)

1. Ask for referrals (5 min)

1. Document insights (8 min)

**When to Escalate to Founder:**

- Problem validation <50% (may need pivot)

- Willingness to pay <30% (pricing issue)

- Customer expressing major dissatisfaction

- Potential new segment with larger opportunity

- Values misalignment with potential customer

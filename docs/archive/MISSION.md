# Indelible Blob: The Mission 🌟

## 👁️ The Vision
To anchor digital reality in silicon and math. We are building the **"Digital Truth Machine"**—a protocol that restores trust to human interaction by proving that what you see is what was captured.

## 📉 The Problem: The Truth Crisis
In an era of ubiquitous GenAI and sophisticated deepfakes, the cost of misinformation is skyrocketing.
*   **Economic Impact**: Estimated **$78 Billion** annually in global misinformation costs.
*   **Eroding Trust**: Digital media (photos, videos, 3D models) is no longer prima facie evidence of reality.

## 🧱 The Solution: Hardware-Backed Authenticity
Indelible Blob moves from "Software-Based Checking" to **"Hardware-Backed Proof."**
*   **Silicon Root of Trust**: Leveraging the **Solana Seeker's TEEPIN** (Trusted Execution Environment) to sign media at the moment of capture.
*   **Privacy-First Verification**: Using **Seal** for decentralized identity-based encryption.
*   **Indelible Storage**: Distributed permanence via the **Walrus Protocol**.
*   **Immutable Notary**: On-chain verification records on the **Sui Blockchain**.

3.  **Real Estate & E-Commerce**: Verifying the physical state of assets.
4.  **Prosumer Content**: Protecting the reputation of authentic creators.

### Growth Tiers

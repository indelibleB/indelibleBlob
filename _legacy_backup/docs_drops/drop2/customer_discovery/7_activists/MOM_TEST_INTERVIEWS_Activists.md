# The Mom Test Interviews: Activists & Whistleblowers Segment
## 5-7 In-Depth Customer Discovery Sessions

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** Activists, NGO directors  
**Goal:** Validate anonymous verification demand, Phase 2 timing

---

## Key Questions

### Documentation Experience Questions

**Q1: "Tell me about a time when you documented misconduct. What happened?"**
- Listen for: specific story, risks taken, outcome

**Q2: "Was your evidence accepted or dismissed? Why?"**
- Listen for: credibility challenges, authenticity questions

**Q3: "Have you faced threats or retaliation for documenting evidence?"**
- Listen for: safety concerns, need for anonymity

---

### Privacy vs. Credibility Questions

**Q4: "How do you currently protect your identity while sharing evidence?"**
- Listen for: metadata stripping, VPNs, Signal, trade-offs

**Q5: "Have you had to choose between anonymity and credibility?"**
- Listen for: specific dilemmas, consequences

---

### Solution Demo

> "We're building anonymous verification using zero-knowledge proofs (Seal). You can prove evidence is authentic WITHOUT revealing your identity. GPS + blockchain verify when/where, but your identity stays private."

**Q6: "Would this have helped in the situation you described?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates solution), concerns

**Q7: "What concerns do you have about this approach?"**
- Listen for: trust in technology, government surveillance, usability

---

### Pricing Questions

**Q8: "Would you pay $4.99/month for anonymous verification?"**
- Listen for: ability to pay (often limited for activists)

**Q9: "For NGOs: Would your organization pay $5k-$20k/year for verified evidence tools?"**
- Listen for: budget availability, decision process

---

### Commitment Test

**Q10: "We're planning to launch Seal integration in 2026. Would you be interested in beta testing?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), safety requirements

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem Severity:** 5/7 have had evidence dismissed
2. **Privacy Need:** 7/7 require anonymity for safety
3. **Solution Fit:** 5/7 would use anonymous verification
4. **NGO Interest:** 3/5 NGOs would pay for organizational license
5. **Beta Interest:** 4/7 would beta test in 2026 (after Seal integration)

# Finding Interest: Scientific Research Segment
## 10 Conversations to Validate Data Integrity Demand

**Target:** Field researchers, environmental scientists, university administrators  
**Goal:** Validate reproducibility crisis creates demand for data verification  
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per conversation

---

## Who to Talk To

- 4 Field Researchers (ecology, geology, climate science)
- 3 GIS Analysts / Spatial Data Scientists
- 2 University Research Administrators / Grant Officers
- 1 Journal Editor / Peer Reviewer

---

## Where to Find Them

### Academic Networks
- **ResearchGate** (social network for scientists)
- **Academia.edu**
- University faculty directories
- Research lab websites

### Conferences
- **AGU** (American Geophysical Union)
- **ESA** (Ecological Society of America)
- **ESRI User Conference** (GIS)

### LinkedIn
- Search: "Environmental Scientist" + University
- Search: "Field Researcher" + [Topic]

---

## Conversation Script

### Opening
> "I'm researching data integrity challenges in field research. Would you have 10 min to chat about data verification? Just learning from researchers—no sales pitch!"

### Key Questions

**Q1: "Do you collect field data (photos, measurements, spatial data) for your research?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates use case), frequency, types

**Q2: "Have reviewers or colleagues ever questioned your data authenticity?"**
- Listen for: specific experiences, how it felt, outcome

**Q3: "How do you currently prove your field data is authentic and unaltered?"**
- Listen for: lab notebooks, metadata, "I don't have a way"

**Q4: "Are you concerned about the reproducibility crisis in your field?"**
- Listen for: YES/NO, personal experiences

**Q5: "Would blockchain-verified field data help with publication or grant applications?"**
- Listen for: YES/NO, specific value

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem:** 7/10 concerned about data integrity
2. **Publication:** 5/10 have had data questioned
3. **Interest:** 6/10 would use data verification tool
4. **Pricing:** 5/10 would pay $19/month (academic budget)
5. **Institutional:** 2/2 universities interested in licensing

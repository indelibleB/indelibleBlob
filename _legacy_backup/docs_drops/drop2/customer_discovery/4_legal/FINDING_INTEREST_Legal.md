# Finding Interest: Legal Professionals Segment
## 10 Conversations to Validate Evidence Verification Demand

**Target:** Criminal/civil attorneys, private investigators  
**Goal:** Validate evidence admissibility challenges create demand  
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per conversation

---

## Who to Talk To

- 4 Criminal Defense Attorneys
- 3 Personal Injury Attorneys
- 2 Private Investigators
- 1 Legal Technology Consultant

---

## Where to Find Them

### LinkedIn
- Search: "Criminal Defense Attorney" + [City]
- Search: "Personal Injury Lawyer" + [City]
- Search: "Private Investigator" + [City]

### Bar Associations
- State bar associations
- Criminal defense associations
- Trial lawyer associations

### Legal Tech Events
- Legaltech conferences
- ABA TECHSHOW
- Legal innovation summits

---

## Conversation Script

### Opening
> "I'm researching evidence verification challenges in legal cases. Would you have 10 minutes to share your experience? Not selling anything—just learning."

### Key Questions

**Q1: "Have you ever had digital evidence (photos/videos) challenged in court?"**
- Listen for: frequency, outcomes, frustration

**Q2: "How do you currently prove evidence authenticity?"**
- Listen for: expert witnesses, forensic analysis, costs

**Q3: "What do expert witnesses cost per case?"**
- Listen for: $5k-$20k range

**Q4: "Would blockchain-verified evidence at capture be valuable?"**
- Listen for: YES/NO, concerns about admissibility

**Q5: "What would you pay monthly for court-admissible evidence verification?"**
- Listen for: $25, $50, $100+ range

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem:** 7/10 have had evidence challenged
2. **Costs:** 5/10 spend $5k+ on expert witnesses
3. **Interest:** 6/10 would use blockchain verification
4. **Pricing:** 5/10 would pay $49/month
5. **Referrals:** 15+ additional attorneys

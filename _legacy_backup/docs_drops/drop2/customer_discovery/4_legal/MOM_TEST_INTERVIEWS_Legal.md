# The Mom Test Interviews: Legal Professionals Segment
## 5-7 In-Depth Customer Discovery Sessions

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** Attorneys, private investigators  
**Goal:** Validate evidence admissibility problem, solution fit, pricing

---

## Key Questions

### Evidence Challenge Questions

**Q1: "Tell me about the last time opposing counsel challenged your digital evidence."**
- Listen for: specific case, what happened, outcome

**Q2: "Walk me through your process for verifying evidence authenticity."**
- Listen for: expert witnesses, forensic tools, costs, timeline

**Q3: "How much have you spent on expert witnesses for evidence verification in the past year?"**
- Listen for: $5k-$50k+ range

---

### Chain of Custody Questions

**Q4: "Have you ever had evidence excluded due to chain of custody issues?"**
- Listen for: specific cases, consequences

**Q5: "How do you currently document chain of custody for digital evidence?"**
- Listen for: manual logs, software, gaps

---

### Solution Demo

> "We're building a tool that verifies evidence at capture using GPS and blockchain. Creates immutable record proving when/where evidence was collected and that it hasn't been tampered with."

**Q6: "Would this have helped in the case you described?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates solution), NO (reveals gaps)

**Q7: "What concerns do you have about blockchain evidence admissibility?"**
- Listen for: legal precedent, jurisdiction, judge acceptance

---

### Pricing Questions

**Q8: "We're considering $49/month for solo practitioners or $5k-$25k/year for law firms. How does that compare to what you spend on expert witnesses?"**
- Listen for: ROI calculation, budget fit

**Q9: "If this reduced expert witness costs by 80%, would you adopt it?"**
- Listen for: commitment level

---

### Commitment Test

**Q10: "Would you use this on your next case requiring evidence verification?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), conditions/requirements

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem Severity:** 5/7 have had evidence challenged, cases affected
2. **Expert Witness Costs:** 5/7 spend $5k+ annually
3. **Solution Fit:** 5/7 would use indelible.Blob for evidence
4. **Pricing:** 4/7 would pay $49/month
5. **Pilot Interest:** 3/7 would use on next case

# Finding Interest: Photographers Segment
## 10 Conversations to Validate Authenticity Demand

**Target:** Professional photographers, stock contributors, photography enthusiasts  
**Goal:** Validate that AI devaluation creates demand for authenticity verification  
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per conversation

---

**Recommended Mix:**
- 4 Professional Photographers (wedding, portrait, commercial)

## Conversation Script (10-15 minutes)

### Opening

> "Thanks for chatting! I'm researching how AI-generated images are affecting photographers. I'm NOT selling anything—just learning from people in the field. Have you noticed AI images impacting your work at all?"

---

### Discovery Questions

**Q1: "Have clients or buyers ever questioned whether your photos are real or AI-generated?"**
- Listen for: specific stories, frequency, how they responded
- **Validates problem exists**

**Q2: "Have you seen AI-generated images competing with authentic photography in your niche?"**
- Listen for: market impact, pricing pressure, lost opportunities
- **Quantifies competition**

**Q3: "Do you contribute to stock photography platforms? Have you had photos rejected for authenticity reasons?"**
- Listen for: Shutterstock/Getty requirements, rejection experiences
- **Validates stock platform pain**

**Q4: "How do you currently prove your photos are authentic and not AI-generated?"**
- Listen for: watermarks, EXIF metadata, "I don't have a way"
- **Reveals solution gap**

**Q5: "If you could prove your photos are authentic with GPS and blockchain verification, would that be valuable?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), MAYBE (lukewarm), NO (not interested)

---

### Interest Gauge

> "I'm working on a tool that verifies photos at capture using GPS and blockchain—proving you were physically there with a camera. It creates an authenticity badge you can show clients and stock platforms. Would you use something like that?"

**If YES:** "What would you pay for it monthly?"  
**If NO:** "What concerns do you have?"

---

### Closing

> "Super helpful! Can I add you to a waiting list for updates? And do you know other photographers I should talk to?"

**After 10 conversations:**

3. **Interest:** 7/10 would use authenticity verification tool

## PROBLEM: What Painful Problem Do They Experience?

### Core Problem Statement

**AI-generated images are flooding the photography market, making it impossible to differentiate authentic photos from synthetic ones. Photographers can't prove their work is real, clients are skeptical, stock platforms are rejecting submissions, and authentic photography is being devalued. Photographers need a way to prove authenticity and protect their work from theft and AI competition.**

---

### Problem Layer 1: AI Devaluation Crisis

**What's Happening:**
- Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion create photorealistic images in seconds
- AI "photographers" undercutting prices (free vs. $500-$5k per shoot)
- Stock libraries flooded with AI images ($1 vs. $50 per photo)
- Clients questioning if photos are real or AI

**Impact:**
- **Financial:** 20-40% revenue decline for professional photographers
- **Reputation:** "Are your photos real or AI?" becoming common question
- **Market:** Authentic photography losing value

**Current Pain Level:** 🔥🔥🔥 **SEVERE** (existential threat to profession)

---

### Problem Layer 2: No Proof of Authenticity

**What's Happening:**
- EXIF metadata can be stripped or faked
- Watermarks can be removed
- No cryptographic proof of original authorship
- Stock platforms can't verify authenticity at scale

**Impact:**
- **Stock contributors:** Photos rejected, income lost
- **Contest entrants:** Disqualified, prizes lost
- **Professionals:** Clients skeptical, bookings lost

**Current Pain Level:** 🔥🔥🔥 **SEVERE** (and getting worse)

---

### Problem Layer 3: Copyright Infringement

**What's Happening:**
- Photos stolen from Instagram, Flickr, portfolios
- Reposted without credit or payment
- Used commercially without license
- No way to prove original ownership

**Impact:**
- **Financial:** Lost licensing revenue ($100-$10k per image)
- **Legal:** Expensive copyright lawsuits ($5k-$50k)
- **Reputation:** Work used without attribution

**Current Pain Level:** 🔥🔥 **HIGH** (ongoing problem)

---

### Problem Layer 4: Stock Platform Requirements

**What's Happening:**
- Shutterstock, Getty, Adobe Stock requiring authenticity verification
- Manual review doesn't scale
- Contributors getting rejected (no proof of authenticity)
- Platforms losing trust as AI images slip through

**Impact:**
- **Contributors:** Income disrupted, photos rejected
- **Platforms:** Reputation damage, customer complaints
- **Buyers:** Can't trust stock photos are authentic

**Current Pain Level:** 🔥🔥 **HIGH** (new requirement, no solution)

---

## SOLUTION: How Does indelible.Blob Solve the Problem?

### Core Solution Statement

**indelible.Blob proves your photos are authentic, not AI-generated. By verifying GPS location, timestamp, and device metadata at the moment of capture, photographers can prove they were physically present with a camera. This differentiates authentic photography from AI-generated images, protects against theft, and meets stock platform authenticity requirements.**

---

### Solution Component 1: Authenticity Badge

**What It Does:**
- Photographer captures photo with indelible.Blob app
- GPS proves they were at location (not AI-generated from home)
- Timestamp proves when photo was taken
- Device metadata proves camera was used (not AI software)
- Blockchain verification creates immutable record

**How It Solves the Problem:**
- **vs. AI images:** AI can't fake GPS + camera metadata
- **vs. skeptical clients:** Show verification badge
- **vs. stock platforms:** Meets authenticity requirements
- **vs. contests:** Prove photo is authentic

**Use Case Example:**
- Emma shoots wedding at vineyard
- Captures photos with indelible.Blob
- GPS shows she was at vineyard ✅
- Timestamp shows photos taken during ceremony ✅

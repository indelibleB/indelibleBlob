# The Mom Test Interviews: Photographers Segment
## 5-7 In-Depth Customer Discovery Sessions

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** Professional photographers, stock contributors  
**Goal:** Validate AI devaluation problem, solution fit, pricing

---

## Interview Structure

### Phase 1: Context (5 min)
### Phase 2: AI Impact (15 min)
### Phase 3: Solution Demo (10 min)
### Phase 4: Pricing & Commitment (10 min)
### Phase 5: Features (5 min)
### Phase 6: Next Steps (5 min)

---

## Key Questions

### AI Impact Questions

**Q1: "Tell me about a time when a client questioned whether your photos were real or AI-generated."**
- Listen for: specific story, how it made them feel, outcome

**Q2: "Have you lost work to AI-generated images or AI 'photographers'?"**
- Listen for: revenue impact, pricing pressure

**Q3: "Walk me through your current workflow for proving authenticity."**
- Listen for: watermarks, metadata, "I don't have a way"

---

### Stock Platform Questions (if applicable)

**Q4: "Have you had photos rejected by stock platforms? Why?"**
- Listen for: authenticity requirements, verification challenges

**Q5: "How much income do you earn from stock photography monthly?"**
- Listen for: $500-$10k range, importance to total income

---

### Solution Demo

> "Let me show you what we're building: You capture photos with our app. GPS proves you were at the location. Blockchain timestamp proves when. Creates an authenticity badge proving it's not AI-generated."

**Q6: "What's your reaction to this?"**
- Listen for: excitement, skepticism, questions

**Q7: "Would this solve the AI skepticism problem you described?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates solution fit), NO (reveals gaps)

---

### Pricing Questions

**Q8: "What would you pay monthly for authenticity verification?"**
- Listen for: $5, $10, $15, $20+ range

**Q9: "We're considering $4.99 for basic (10 photos/month) and $14.99 for pro (unlimited). How does that sound?"**
- Listen for: too expensive, reasonable, cheap

---

### Commitment Test

**Q10: "Would you sign up for a free trial next month?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), MAYBE (lukewarm)

**Q11: "If the free trial proves useful, would you upgrade to paid?"**
- Listen for: commitment level, requirements

---

## Success Criteria

**After 5-7 interviews:**

1. **Problem Severity:** 5/7 have experienced AI skepticism or lost work
2. **Solution Fit:** 5/7 say indelible.Blob solves their problem
3. **Pricing:** 4/7 would pay $4.99-$14.99/month
4. **Trial Interest:** 5/7 would try free trial
5. **Upgrade Intent:** 3/7 would upgrade to paid after trial

## Document Structure

Each segment contains three documents:

1. **UPS_MODEL_[Segment].md** - User-Problem-Solution framework
   - User personas (3-5 detailed profiles)
   - Problem breakdown (4 layers: core, urgency, impact, current solutions)
   - Solution components (how indelible.Blob solves their problems)
   - Competitive differentiation
   - 5 testable hypotheses
   - Success criteria

2. **FINDING_INTEREST_[Segment].md** - 10 Conversations guide
   - Target profile distribution
   - Where to find them (communities, platforms, associations)
   - Conversation script (10-15 minutes)
   - Outreach templates
   - Success criteria

3. **MOM_TEST_INTERVIEWS_[Segment].md** - 5-7 In-depth sessions
   - 6-phase interview structure (45-60 minutes)
   - Segment-specific questions (past behavior focus)
   - Demo flow
   - Commitment test questions
   - Success criteria

## How to Use These Materials

### Step 1: Choose Your Segment Priority
Based on your phase roadmap:
- **Phase 1A (Now):** Journalism (already have materials)
- **Phase 1B (Months 7-12):** Insurance + Photographers
- **Phase 1C (Months 13-24):** Legal
- **Phase 2 (Months 25-36):** Creative Industries + Scientific + Activists + AEC

### Step 2: Start with "Finding Interest" (10 Conversations)
- Use outreach templates to contact 10 people
- Follow conversation script (10-15 minutes each)
- Track results in conversation tracker
- Validate problem severity and initial interest

### Step 3: Move to "The Mom Test" (5-7 In-Depth Interviews)
- Select most promising contacts from Step 2
- Schedule 45-60 minute interviews
- Follow 6-phase interview structure
- Test commitment with real actions (not hypotheticals)

### Step 4: Evaluate Success Criteria
- Each segment has 5 success criteria
- If 4-5 criteria met: Proceed with product development
- If 2-3 criteria met: Refine and do more interviews
- If 0-1 criteria met: Pivot to different segment or solution

### Step 5: Iterate Based on Learnings
- Document surprises and insights
- Update UPS Model based on feedback
- Refine pricing based on willingness to pay
- Adjust feature priorities based on must-haves

## Key Principles (The Mom Test)

✅ **Talk about their life, not your idea**  
✅ **Ask about specifics in the past, not generics or opinions about the future**  
✅ **Talk less, listen more**  
✅ **Ask for commitment, not compliments**  
✅ **Advance to the next step (referrals, pilot, payment)**

❌ **Don't pitch your solution in the first 5 minutes**  
❌ **Don't ask leading questions ("Wouldn't it be great if...")**  
❌ **Don't argue with objections**  
❌ **Don't talk to friends and family (they'll be too nice)**

# The Mom Test Interviews: Creative Industries Segment
## 5-7 In-Depth Customer Discovery Sessions

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** VFX artists, production companies  
**Goal:** Validate spatial data marketplace demand, pricing, Phase 2 timing

---

## Key Questions

### Spatial Data Usage Questions

**Q1: "Tell me about the last project where you needed 3D spatial data of a real location."**
- Listen for: specific project, what data needed, how obtained

**Q2: "Walk me through your process for getting spatial data. What worked? What didn't?"**
- Listen for: DIY scanning, stock libraries, costs, quality issues

**Q3: "How much did that spatial data cost? Time and money?"**
- Listen for: $500-$50k range, weeks/months of work

---

### Quality & Verification Questions

**Q4: "Have you ever used spatial data that turned out to be inaccurate or unusable?"**
- Listen for: specific problems, consequences, wasted investment

**Q5: "How do you verify spatial data quality before using it in production?"**
- Listen for: manual checks, no verification, trust the source

---

### Solution Demo

> "We're building verified spatial data capture using GPS + blockchain. Creators scan locations with LiDAR, we verify authenticity and accuracy. Marketplace lets you license verified scans with clear rights."

**Q6: "Would verified spatial data have helped on the project you described?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates solution), specific value

**Q7: "Would you create and license your own spatial scans on this marketplace?"**
- Listen for: creator interest, pricing expectations

---

### Pricing Questions

**Q8: "What would you pay monthly for access to a verified spatial data library?"**
- Listen for: $25, $50, $100+ range

**Q9: "For production companies: What would you pay annually for unlimited verified spatial data?"**
- Listen for: $50k, $100k, $500k+ range

---

### Commitment Test

**Q10: "We're planning to launch 3D spatial capture in 2026. Would you be interested in beta testing?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), requirements

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem Severity:** 5/7 have spatial data quality/licensing issues
2. **Solution Fit:** 5/7 would use verified spatial data marketplace
3. **Pricing (B2C):** 4/7 would pay $25-$50/month
4. **Pricing (B2B):** 2/3 production companies would pay $50k-$500k/year
5. **Beta Interest:** 4/7 would beta test in 2026

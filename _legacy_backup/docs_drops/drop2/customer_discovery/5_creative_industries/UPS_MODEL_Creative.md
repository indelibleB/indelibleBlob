- Creates digital environments for film/TV

- Works with Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender

### Persona 2: 3D Environment Artist / Spatial Computing Creator
- Builds AR/VR experiences, metaverse environments

### Persona 3: Production Company / Film Studio
- Produces films, TV shows, commercials
- Needs verified spatial data library
- Budget: $1M-$100M+ per production
- Virtual production workflows (LED walls + Unreal Engine)
- Location scouting costs $50k-$500k per production

**Pain:** Expensive location scouting, need verified spatial data, licensing and rights management

---

## PROBLEM: What Painful Problem Do They Experience?

### Core Problem

**Film and spatial computing creators need verified 3D spatial data of real-world locations for virtual production, but there's no way to prove authenticity, provenance, or licensing rights. Location scouting is expensive ($50k-$500k per production), spatial scans are unreliable, and licensing disputes cost millions. Creators need a verified spatial data marketplace.**

---

### Problem Layers

**Layer 1: Verified Spatial Data Gap**
- No marketplace for verified 3D scans of locations
- Quality inconsistent (amateur scans vs. professional)
- No proof of authenticity (could be AI-generated or manipulated)
- **Impact:** Productions waste time/money on unreliable data

**Layer 2: Location Scouting Costs**
- Physical location scouting costs $50k-$500k per production
- Travel, permits, crew time
- Virtual production could reduce costs 80%
- **Impact:** Budget waste, production delays

**Layer 3: Licensing & Rights Management**
- Who owns the spatial scan? The creator? The property owner?
- Licensing terms unclear
- Disputes cost $100k-$1M+ in legal fees
- **Impact:** Legal risk, production delays

**Layer 4: Spatial Data Quality Assurance**
- No verification of GPS accuracy
- No timestamp proof (scan could be outdated)
- No chain of custody (data could be altered)
- **Impact:** Unusable spatial data, wasted investment

---

## SOLUTION: How Does indelible.Blob Solve the Problem?

### Core Solution

**indelible.Blob verifies 3D spatial data at capture using GPS, blockchain, and Walrus storage. Creators can prove WHERE the scan was taken, WHEN, and that it hasn't been altered. Phase 3 marketplace enables licensing with smart contracts, royalties, and attribution. This creates a verified spatial data library for virtual production.**

---

### Solution Components

**1. Verified 3D Spatial Data Capture (Phase 2)**
- LiDAR integration (iPhone Pro, dedicated scanners)
- Photogrammetry support
- GPS + orientation verification
- Blockchain timestamp
- Walrus storage for large 3D files
- **Benefit:** Provably authentic spatial data

**2. Spatial Data Marketplace (Phase 3)**
- Creators upload verified 3D scans
- Buyers (production companies, game studios) license data
- Smart contracts handle payments, royalties, attribution
- 10-20% commission for indelible.Blob
- **Benefit:** Monetize spatial scans, clear licensing

**3. Production Cost Savings**
- Virtual location scouting vs. physical ($5k vs. $50k)
- Verified spatial data library (no travel needed)
- Faster pre-production (weeks vs. months)
- **Benefit:** 80% cost reduction, faster timelines

**4. API Integration**
- Unreal Engine plugin
- Unity integration
- Blender add-on
- Direct import of verified spatial data
- **Benefit:** Seamless workflow integration

---

**vs. Physical Location Scouting:** $5k vs. $50k-$500k  
**vs. Amateur Spatial Scans:** Professional quality + verification

# Finding Interest: Creative Industries Segment
## 10 Conversations to Validate Spatial Data Demand

**Target:** VFX artists, 3D designers, production companies  
**Goal:** Validate need for verified spatial data marketplace  
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per conversation

---

## Who to Talk To

- 4 VFX Artists / Virtual Production Designers
- 3 3D Environment Artists / Spatial Computing Creators
- 2 Production Company Executives / Location Managers
- 1 Game Studio Technical Director

---

## Where to Find Them

### Communities
- **SIGGRAPH** (annual conference, online community)
- **GDC** (Game Developers Conference)
- **Unreal Engine forums**
- **Unity forums**
- **Blender Artists community**
- **r/vfx** (Reddit)
- **r/unrealengine** (Reddit)

### LinkedIn
- Search: "VFX Artist" + [Company]
- Search: "Virtual Production" + [Studio]
- Target companies: ILM, Weta Digital, Framestore, MPC

---

## Conversation Script

### Opening
> "I'm researching spatial data needs for virtual production. Would you have 10 min to chat about location scanning and verification? Just learning—no sales pitch!"

### Key Questions

**Q1: "Do you use 3D scans of real-world locations in your work?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates use case), how often, what for

**Q2: "Where do you currently get spatial data? What's frustrating about it?"**
- Listen for: DIY scans, stock libraries, quality issues, licensing unclear

**Q3: "Have you ever had issues with spatial data authenticity or quality?"**
- Listen for: specific problems, wasted time/money

**Q4: "Would you pay for verified, high-quality spatial scans with clear licensing?"**
- Listen for: YES/NO, price sensitivity

**Q5: "What would you pay monthly for access to a verified spatial data library?"**
- Listen for: $25, $50, $100+ range

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem:** 7/10 use spatial data, have quality/licensing issues
2. **Interest:** 6/10 would use verified spatial data marketplace
3. **Pricing:** 5/10 would pay $25-$50/month
4. **Enterprise:** 2/2 production companies interested in $50k-$500k/year licensing
5. **Timing:** 6/10 would adopt when 3D capture available (2026)

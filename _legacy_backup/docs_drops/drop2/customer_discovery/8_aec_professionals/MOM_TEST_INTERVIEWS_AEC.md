# The Mom Test Interviews: AEC Professionals Segment
## 5-7 In-Depth Customer Discovery Sessions

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Target:** Construction managers, engineers, architects  
**Goal:** Validate documentation disputes problem, solution fit, pricing

---

## Key Questions

### Dispute Experience Questions

**Q1: "Tell me about the most significant construction dispute you've been involved in. What was disputed?"**
- Listen for: specific project, documentation issues, costs, outcome

**Q2: "Walk me through how documentation was used (or challenged) in that dispute."**
- Listen for: photos questioned, timestamps disputed, expert witnesses needed

**Q3: "How much did that dispute cost? Time and money?"**
- Listen for: $50k-$5M range, months/years of delays

---

### Liability Questions

**Q4: "Have you faced professional liability claims? Tell me about that."**
- Listen for: site condition disputes, design vs. as-built, insurance claims

**Q5: "How do you currently protect yourself from liability with documentation?"**
- Listen for: photos, 3D scans, manual logs, professional practices

---

### As-Built Documentation Questions

**Q6: "Do you use 3D scanning for as-built documentation? What's frustrating about it?"**
- Listen for: accuracy issues, verification challenges, BIM coordination

---

### Solution Demo

> "We're building verified site documentation using GPS + blockchain. Proves when/where photos and 3D scans were captured. Creates court-admissible evidence for disputes."

**Q7: "Would this have helped in the dispute you described?"**
- Listen for: YES (validates solution), specific value

**Q8: "Would verified documentation reduce your professional liability risk?"**
- Listen for: insurance implications, peace of mind

---

### Pricing Questions

**Q9: "What do expert witnesses cost in construction disputes?"**
- Listen for: $5k-$20k per dispute

**Q10: "We're considering $29/month for individuals or $10k-$100k/year for firms/projects. How does that compare?"**
- Listen for: ROI calculation, budget fit

---

### Commitment Test

**Q11: "Would you use this on your next construction project?"**
- Listen for: YES (strong signal), requirements

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem Severity:** 5/7 have faced costly documentation disputes
2. **Liability:** 5/7 have faced liability claims
3. **Solution Fit:** 5/7 would use indelible.Blob for site documentation
4. **Pricing:** 4/7 would pay $29/month
5. **Next Project:** 4/7 would use on next construction project

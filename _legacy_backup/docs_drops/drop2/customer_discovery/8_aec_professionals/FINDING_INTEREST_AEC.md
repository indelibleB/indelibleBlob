# Finding Interest: AEC Professionals Segment
## 10 Conversations to Validate Construction Documentation Demand

**Target:** Construction managers, engineers, architects  
**Goal:** Validate construction disputes create demand for verified documentation  
**Time:** 10-15 minutes per conversation

---

## Who to Talk To

- 4 Construction Managers / Site Supervisors
- 3 Structural/Civil Engineers
- 2 Architects / BIM Managers
- 1 Construction Attorney / Claims Consultant

---

## Where to Find Them

### Professional Associations
- **AGC** (Associated General Contractors)
- **ASCE** (American Society of Civil Engineers)
- **AIA** (American Institute of Architects)
- **CMAA** (Construction Management Association)

### LinkedIn
- Search: "Construction Manager" + [Company]
- Search: "Civil Engineer" + [Firm]
- Search: "BIM Manager" + [Architecture Firm]

### Events
- **World of Concrete**
- **AIA Conference**
- **Autodesk University** (BIM/Revit users)

---

## Conversation Script

### Opening
> "I'm researching documentation challenges in construction projects. Would you have 10 min to chat about site documentation and disputes? Just learning from people in the field—no sales pitch!"

### Key Questions

**Q1: "Have you ever been involved in a construction dispute where documentation was questioned?"**
- Listen for: specific project, what was disputed, outcome

**Q2: "How do you currently document site conditions and progress?"**
- Listen for: photos, 3D scans, manual logs, software (Procore, PlanGrid)

**Q3: "Have you faced liability claims requiring proof of site conditions?"**
- Listen for: professional liability, insurance claims, costs

**Q4: "Would blockchain-verified site documentation help with disputes or liability?"**
- Listen for: YES/NO, concerns

**Q5: "What would you pay monthly for verified, court-admissible documentation?"**
- Listen for: $25, $50, $100+ range

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Problem:** 7/10 have faced documentation disputes
2. **Liability:** 6/10 have faced liability claims
3. **Interest:** 6/10 would use verified documentation
4. **Pricing:** 5/10 would pay $29/month
5. **Firm Interest:** 2/2 firms interested in $10k-$100k/year project licensing
